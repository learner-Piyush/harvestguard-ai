# HarvestGuard AI Design System

This document outlines the design theme, typography, color palette, and screens for the **HarvestGuard AI** project (`projects/9468321582405428393`).

---

## Brand & Style
The design system is built to evoke a sense of **precision, vitality, and reliability**. As a produce monitoring tool, the UI balances technical AI capabilities with the organic nature of agriculture. 

The aesthetic follows a **Modern Organic** direction—mixing clean, functional layouts with soft, natural geometries. It prioritize high legibility and a calm emotional response, ensuring that users feel in control of their inventory. The style avoids "tech-coldness" by using warm neutrals and generous whitespace, creating an approachable but professional tool for farmers, grocers, and distributors.

---

## Color Palette

### Core Colors
* **Primary (Forest Green)**: `#154212` (Container: `#2d5a27`, On-Primary: `#ffffff`, On-Primary-Container: `#9dd090`)
  * *Used for core navigation, primary actions, and representing "Optimal Freshness". It conveys stability and growth.*
* **Secondary (Clementine)**: `#934b00` (Container: `#fc943b`, On-Secondary: `#ffffff`, On-Secondary-Container: `#683300`)
  * *A warm, non-aggressive orange used for warnings, ripening alerts, and secondary calls to action. It stands out against the green without creating panic.*
* **Tertiary (Sapling Green)**: `#2e3e00` (Container: `#425700`, On-Tertiary: `#ffffff`, On-Tertiary-Container: `#a6d12c`)
  * *A brighter, energetic green used for data visualizations and success states.*

## Typography

This design system utilizes a dual-font strategy. **Montserrat** provides a geometric, confident structure for headlines, giving the brand a modern "agri-tech" voice. **Inter** is used for all functional body text and data points due to its exceptional legibility at small sizes.

* **Headline Large (Desktop)**
  * **Font Family**: Montserrat
  * **Font Size**: `32px`
  * **Font Weight**: `700` (Bold)
  * **Line Height**: `40px`
  * **Letter Spacing**: `-0.02em`
* **Headline Large (Mobile)**
  * **Font Family**: Montserrat
  * **Font Size**: `24px`
  * **Font Weight**: `700` (Bold)
  * **Line Height**: `32px`
  * **Letter Spacing**: `-0.01em`
* **Headline Medium**
